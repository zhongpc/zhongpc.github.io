Welcome! I am a postdoctoral researcher in [Ceder Group](https://ceder.berkeley.edu/) @ Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. I am interested in solving energy & sustainability challenges by combining computational modeling, machine learning, and materials science.

**Previously**: I obtained my Ph.D. in Materials Science from UC Berkeley in August 2023. Prior to Berkeley, I obtained my B.S. in physics from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2018.

My research includes
* Computational modeling of energy and sustainable materials from first principles
* Statistical mechanics and electronic structure methods in solid-state materials
* AI for Science: atomistic modeling and generative models in physics/chemistry/materials
