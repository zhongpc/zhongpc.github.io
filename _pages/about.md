Hello, I am a fourth-year Ph.D student in [Ceder Group](https://ceder.berkeley.edu/) @ [UC Berkeley](https://mse.berkeley.edu/). 
I obtained my BS in physics from [University of Science and Technology of China](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/University_of_Science_and_Technology_of_China). 

Currently, my research focus on:

* Computational design of energy storage materials from first-principle (Li-ion batteries) 
* Statistical mechanics on lattices and disordered systems
* Mathematical programming and optimization for robust computational modeling
* Machine learning approaches for data interpretation in materials science

See [Research](../research/index.html) page for details.
